BLOG 2:The use of humour in Trevor Noah's Life.

Trevor Noah uses humour to illustrate how his relationship
with his mum was like Tom and Jerry.He explains how his mum was a strict
disciplinarian and would give hiding through throwing things at Trevor and
chasing while screaming "Stop Thief!" for everyone to grab him and tackle him.


Trevor Noah uses humour to illustrate the seriousness women in his family
put in God and worship.He sarcarstically paints how the women end their song with
"Ameeeeeeen","Ameeeeeeeeeeeeeeeeeeen" Trevor Noah illustrates how his mother reacted after he went to the salon and got his hair done.She said he looked
like the most beautiful boy she has ever seen.She sarcastically stated that she did not want competition. Trevor Noah uses humour to show the his interaction with students while in school.He says,"The good looking
guy will always win the girl.The funny guy will basically make everyone laugh and have no girlfriend.


Trevor Noah depicts humour when he was faced with a dilemna in hospital
to either pay his mothers hospital bill or hope she will recover from the gunshot.He states that
what if he spends all his money and his mother wakes up from a comma and say,"how much did you spend?
